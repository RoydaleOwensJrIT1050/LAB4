General Information

The city of Omen is a wonderful place to call your own home, people from all over the world comes to visit Omen. The city of Omen is approximately 82.48 mi2 with the population 192,000 people. The cost of living is not too high if your monthly income is at least $1,600 you will be able to make it here. The crime rate is high but not unavailable to live in peace. The weather can be changed but Omen goes through four seasons. A vehicle is needed for most people, but you can travel to a few places by foot. The skies and air are pretty clear some areas maybe cleaner than others.
